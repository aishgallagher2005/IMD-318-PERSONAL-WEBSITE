🏡 Meet My Family

"Family is where our story begins and love never ends." Here is a small tribute to my home circle.

❤️ My Dearest Father

My father is the anchor of peace in our home. A man of quiet strength, he is firm but rarely ever raises his voice, teaching me the value of patience through his actions rather than words. The moments I treasure most are when we sit down together at night after a long day, just sharing simple stories about life while enjoying the cool night breeze. His life advice always serves as a reliable compass for me to stay grounded.

💖 My Beautiful Mother

My mother is the true definition of a 'safe haven.' Her endless love and care always manage to ease all my cluttered thoughts after returning home from campus. Her cooking is what I miss the most whenever I am away, especially the simple comfort food she makes with absolute sincerity. Her smile is my greatest motivation, always making me believe that everything will eventually be alright.

💪 My First Younger Brother

As my first younger brother, he carries a great sense of responsibility and sets an amazing example for the rest of us. Even while keeping busy with his own growing path, he always finds time to check in on how I am doing at UiTM. Our childhood memories of sharing toys and old hobbies built a strong foundation for a brotherhood that remains unbreakable to this day.

✨ My Second Younger Brother

My second younger brother has a incredibly laid-back and bright personality. He is often the mood-maker in our family, effortlessly making everyone laugh with his casual wit. Behind his funny nature, he is an excellent conversationalist when it comes to sharing common interests, especially music, anime, and creative designs. His presence keeps our family dynamic lively and never boring.

🎀 My Youngest Brother

Our youngest brother brings a youthful energy to the house that constantly reminds me of my own childhood days. Watching him grow up with his own unique interests and hobbies makes me realize just how fast time flies. Although we occasionally have our disagreements, we always silently support one another. Being an older brother to him teaches me the true meaning of responsibility and being a protective guide.
