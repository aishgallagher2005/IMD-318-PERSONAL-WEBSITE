KINDERGARTEN PRIMARY SCHOOL SECONDARY SCHOOL DIPLOMA

[ 01. KINDERGARTEN ]

My kindergarten days were my very first step into the social world. Even back then, I preferred spending my time peacefully coloring in the corner of the classroom rather than playing rough outside. One of my funniest and most unforgettable memories is constantly falling asleep at my desk during afternoon reading sessions because the classroom atmosphere was just so dim and comforting.

[ 02. PRIMARY SCHOOL ]

As I moved on to primary school, my passion for creative subjects began to grow, especially in Art Education. I was always excited whenever drawing assignments were given. Amidst the busy school routine, finding a quiet moment in the peaceful library to read storybooks served as my ultimate 'escape zone' from the loud crowd.

[ 03. SECONDARY SCHOOL ]

In high school, my personality as an INFP became much more apparent. The most meaningful experience during this phase was curating my own music playlists—mostly consisting of emo and slow rock tracks—to keep me company during late-night study sessions for the SPM examination. Even while focusing on my studies, I made sure my lifestyle remained steady, slow-paced, and stress-free.

[ 04. DIPLOMA IN UITM ]

Now, life as a Diploma student at UiTM has given me a wider space to explore the digital landscape. Balancing my IMD318 course work with campus life is teaching me how to be truly independent. Despite the hectic schedule of lectures and assignments, I always find time for a quick 'healing' session—reading a book, watching anime, or vibing to my favorite tracks to keep my life beautifully balanced.
